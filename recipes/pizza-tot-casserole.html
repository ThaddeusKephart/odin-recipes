Pizza Tator Tot Casserole

Description

Imagine crispy, golden tater tots forming a delightful crust, cradling a savory, seasoned ground beef filling. This Pizza Tater Tot Casserole is a comforting and crowd-pleasing dish that combines the best of two worlds. 1 A rich tomato sauce, studded with tender onions, vibrant green peppers, and a hidden boost of spinach, melds with the browned ground beef, creating a hearty base. This flavorful mixture is then nestled between layers of perfectly baked tater tots, their crispy exteriors providing a satisfying crunch with every bite. Finally, a generous blanket of melted mozzarella cheese tops it all off, adding a gooey, cheesy finish that pulls it all together. This easy-to-make casserole is a guaranteed hit for weeknight dinners or casual gatherings, offering a fun and delicious twist on classic comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 pounds ground beef
  • Optional: 1 medium green pepper, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1/2 pound sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 can (15 ounces) pizza sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 3 cups shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese
  • 1 package (32 ounces) frozen Tater Tots
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. In a large skillet, cook the beef, green pepper if desired, onion and mushrooms over medium heat until meat is no longer pink, breaking meat into crumbles; drain. Add pizza sauce and basil.
  2. Transfer to a greased 3-qt. or 13x9-in. baking dish. Top with mozzarella cheese and potatoes. Bake, uncovered, at 400Â° until potatoes are lightly browned, 30-35 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese; bake until cheese is melted, about 5 minutes.
